Frequently Asked Questions
Tap Sell in the bottom navigation bar. Fill in the part details, add photos, set your price and tap Publish. Your listing will appear live on APC immediately.
If a seller has enabled offers on their listing, you'll see a Make an Offer button. Submit your offer and the seller can accept, decline or counter it. All offer activity appears in your Messages inbox.
Tap Contact Seller on any listing to start a conversation. You'll need to be signed in. All your conversations are in your Messages inbox, accessible from the nav bar.
Yes. Tap the Filter button and select your Make, Model and Year. Results will narrow to parts listed as compatible with your vehicle.
Delivery is arranged directly between buyer and seller via the chat. Check the listing for postage and pickup availability, then use the messaging to confirm the details and payment.
Open the listing and scroll to the bottom — you'll find a Report Listing option. Alternatively, contact us via the form below and include the listing details. We review all reports promptly.
APC Pro is designed for businesses — wrecking yards, dealers and auto workshops. It unlocks bulk listing tools, a Pro Dashboard with analytics, stock management, warehouse bin tracking and more. Contact us to find out about upgrading.
Send us a message using the contact form below with your registered email address and we'll take care of it within 2 business days.
